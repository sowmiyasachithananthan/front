Weight training helps build muscle strength, improve endurance, and enhance overall body performance. Our trainers guide members through structured strength training programs.

CrossFit combines functional movements with high intensity training to improve endurance, agility and strength.

  • Treadmill running
  • Cycling workouts
  • HIIT training
  • Endurance building
